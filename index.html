Learn how to create a Password

With hackers becoming more and more cunning in their methods and attempt to steal your data, it is more,important than ever to use “secure passwords” that make it difficult for crackers to break.


Not only that,it is imperative that you use a range of different secure passwords for different sites. This is because in the event of one of your passwords is compromised, the others will remain safe.

Passwords are everywhere and here are some short rules to remember when creating a password


  • The password should be 8 or more characters long

  • Longer passwords are better than shorter passwords

  • Alternate upper and lowercase letters

  • Use symbols like # in your password.

  • Passwords should not be made up of dictionnary words names or dates
